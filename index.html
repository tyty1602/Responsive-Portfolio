About me

I’m Lan. I live in Seattle with my husband Michael and my Shih Tzu Coconut Chip. That is the most important fact about me but if you’re interested in the details, see below:

I grew up in Vietnam until I was 16 years old. Then I came to Michigan as a high school exchange student and ended up finishing my degree in Supply Chain Management from Michigan State University (GO GREEN!). I have always worked a small job and taken internship during the summer for most of my college career. In addition to my GPA of 3.8, I believe those internships with HP and MeadWestvaco helped me scored a job with Microsoft in Seattle. I worked at Microsoft for about 3.5 years and learned some epic lessons and made some rookie mistake during a holiday season (Eeek! No worries, mistake was fixed and lesson was learned). I learned that old school manufacturing wasn’t for me so I took a chance and got a job at Amazon.com in 2012. I still often asked myself how they hired me (lol). But lucky for them, I delivered a bunch of results and worked on projects from fulfillment cost reduction to launching new Prime Now cities and managing a billion plus category. Anyhow, you’d think I’d stay as a business woman for the rest of my life benefiting from the company brand name and the golden handcuff (holy cow Amazon stocks!). But no, here I am learning how to code and I’m regularly banging my head and yell into empty space in an attempt to push myself.

Cheers to the unknown future ahead of me and of us, my classmates. But hey, we’ll figure it out.
